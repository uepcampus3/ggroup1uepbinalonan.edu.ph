About Our University

The University of Eastern Pangasinan (UEP) is a higher education institution located in Binalonan, Pangasinan, Philippines. Established in 2005 through a public-private partnership led by local government officials, the university was created to make quality tertiary education more accessible to students in Eastern Pangasinan and nearby areas.

UEP offers a variety of undergraduate and diploma programs across different fields such as education, business administration, information technology, criminology, hospitality management, and health-related courses like midwifery. Its academic programs are designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for professional careers and community service.
